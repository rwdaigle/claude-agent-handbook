There may not be industry consensus on the following terms, but these are the definitions assumed for the purposes of this handbook.

## Agent
A system operating with autonomy that is capable of organizing its work, interacting with its environment, and determining its work completion in the pursuit of a specified outcome.

## Workflow
A pre-defined graph of computations, connections, and control flow.

## Subagent
An agent executing some scope of work within the context and control of a parent agent.

## Tool
Any pre-defined capability given to an agent for interacting with its environment.
